About

A mentor once told that me in order to become skilled at anything, you have to try it, and you have to be bad at it. That was my barrier into design and development for years - the fear of being bad. Many sleepless nights and duct-tape websites later, I've amassed a considerable skill-set in code to complement a strong background in journalism. I'm obsessive about typography, code semantically in both HTML5 and div-based layouts and am constantly experimenting with alternative page designs. I also prefer amber beers and am a fantasy sports junkie. Below you'll see I'm also on the social media, and have regularly updated Github and Dribbble accounts.You can email me here.
